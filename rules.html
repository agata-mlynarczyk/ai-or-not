📜 Rules

Do you think you are an expert at distinguishing human generated text from AI?
The rules are simple.
You need to decide if the text was generated by a human or AI.
Look at the categories above the text to get some context.
For each correct answer you gain 1 point.
For each incorrect answer you lose 1 point.
Check how good you are in the leaderboard!
